# Pioneer Profile — Alfie Correa

## What are you building?

I am building Factory3: a modular runtime that lets any business deploy AI-powered chat agents, lead capture flows, operations dashboards and campaign automations — without starting from scratch each time.

The core insight is that most small business automation projects share the same structure: a company config, an AI prompt layer, a data model, and a way to expose it via bot or dashboard. Factory3 abstracts that into skills, bots and company modules that can be composed and deployed on Render in hours, not weeks.

Right now Factory3 powers 4 live client systems: a freight logistics operation, a marketing agency chat agent, a recruiting dashboard and a real estate campaign system. The next milestone is turning Factory3 into a service I can offer on Upwork and through Estoiko Lab — an agency partner who sells AI chat agents to her clients using my stack.

## Why now?

Small businesses know they need AI but do not know where to start. Most AI tools either require technical teams or are too generic to fit real operations. There is a clear gap for someone who can take a business process — a sales conversation, a trip log, a job vacancy — and turn it into a working automated system in a week.

The clients who need this most are marketing agencies, real estate teams, local service businesses. They do not need a SaaS subscription; they need one person who understands their workflow and ships something they can actually use. Factory3 is built exactly for that: composable, deployable, and easy to explain to a non-technical client.
